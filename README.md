Aperion — Focus OS (Origin + Flow)
Aperion is a comprehensive "Focus Operating System" designed to transform deep work from a fleeting state into a measurable, repeatable skill. Unlike traditional timers or website blockers, Aperion acts as a cognitive companion that calibrates your readiness, prescribes optimal work sessions based on your biological resource levels, and protects your focus through enforced commitment.

The Philosophy: Focus as a Resource
Aperion is built on the truth that deep work is a skill being lost to digital drift. Most tools rely on willpower, which is finite and fluctuating. Aperion shifts the paradigm by:

Treating focus as a resource: Sprints are sized according to your sleep and stress levels.

Calibration over Motivation: You don't just "start" a session; you earn it through cognitive "Gatekeeping."

Reality vs. Perception: The system provides "receipts" of your actual focus time versus perceived effort.

Features & Modules
1. The Focus Gatekeeper (Entry Ritual)
Deep work requires a state shift. To ensure you are cognitively ready, Aperion requires you to pass two calibration steps:

The Purge: A reaction-timing drill designed to clear mental noise and lock attention.

Stroop Test: A cognitive interference task to measure and prime your executive control.

2. Vibe Check & The Echo (Planning)
Once calibrated, the system helps you choose the right depth for your current state:

Vibe Check: Predicts focus capacity by analyzing sleep and stress inputs to recommend session lengths.

The Echo: A cockpit-style pre-flight checklist that prescribes specific sprint types and environments based on your goals.

3. Focus Vault (Execution)
A commitment device for your deep work sessions. The Vault moves beyond soft timers:

Enforced Sessions: A self-contract mechanism where exiting a session requires specific conditions to be met.

Session Protection: Actively guards your time against the "drift" of tabs and notifications.

4. The Dashboard (Reflection)
A data-driven overview of your focus patterns. It tracks:

Honest Signals: Sessions started vs. completed.

The Reality Gap: Average minutes of actual focus vs. self-perception.

Biometric Trends: Correlations between sleep, vibes logged, and plan adherence.

The Product Loop
Gatekeeper: Pass calibration to unlock focus modules.

Vibe Check / Echo: Receive a personalized sprint prescription.

Focus Vault: Enter session mode with enforced protection.

Dashboard: Reflect on patterns and improve for the next cycle.

Tech Stack
Frontend: Built with modern web technologies to ensure a seamless, "OS-like" interface.

State Management: Complex logic for handling session states, calibration scoring, and user inputs.

Cognitive Testing: Custom implementations of the Stroop Effect and reaction drills.

One-Line Summary
Aperion is the Focus OS implemented as Calibration → Personalized Sprint Prescription → Enforcement → Reflection.
